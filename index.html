Kalpana Chawla

Kalpana Chawla was a remarkable astronaut and aerospace engineer
who made history as the first woman of Indian origin in space.
Her achievements continue to inspire generations.

Born on March 17, 1962, in Karnal, India, she pursued her passion for aerospace engineering and became a NASA astronaut.
She participated in two space missions, including the tragic STS-107 mission.

Her dedication, courage, and contributions to space exploration remain a lasting legacy.
